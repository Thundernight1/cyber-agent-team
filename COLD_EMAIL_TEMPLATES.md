## SENDING STRATEGY

### Tool: Hunter.io or Apollo.io
- Find company emails
- Get decision-maker contacts
- Cost: $100-300/month

### Schedule:
- Monday-Thursday only (Friday = low open rates)
- 9-10am or 4-5pm (business hours)
- Space out: 30 emails/day max (don't spam filter)
- Follow up after 4-5 days if no response

### Tracking:
- Use: HubSpot (free), Outreach, or even Google Sheets
- Track: Open rate, reply rate, meetings booked
- Optimize: Kill emails under 5% response rate
